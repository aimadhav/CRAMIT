PYQ 2021 HARD

Question

Two point charges \( q_1 \) and \( q_2 \) are placed at a distance \( r \). What is the magnitude of the electrostatic force between them in a vacuum?

Tap to view diagram

Answer

$$ F = k \frac{|q_1 q_2|}{r^2} $$

Where \( k \) is Coulomb's constant \( k \approx 9 \times 10^9 \, \text{N}\cdot\text{m}^2/\text{C}^2 \).

CramIt Key Concept

The force is attractive if charges have opposite signs and repulsive if they have the same sign. It follows an inverse square law.
